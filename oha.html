OHA

Nigerian Soup










INGREDIENTS



  1. Vegetable:Ora leaves
  2. 8 small corms cocoyam
  3. Assorted Beef: Includes best cut, shaki (cow tripe)
  4. 1-2 Chopped onions
  5. 3-4Scotch Bonnet Pepper
  6. Grounded Crayfish
  7. 3cups of palm oil
  8. Stock Fish
  9. Seasoning cubes
  10. Salt
  11. 1 teaspoon Ogiri Igbo






How To Prepare



  1. Boil the shaki (cow tripe), stock fish and dry fish in 1 litre of water till they are well done. First sign of a done shaki is that the cuts will start curling on itself. Wash the beef and add to the pot of shaki etc. and continue cooking. When the meat is done, add 2 stock cubes and cook for 5 minutes.

  2. Add the pepper, ogiri Igbo and ground crayfish and cook for 10 minutes. Add the cocoyam paste in small lumps and then the palm oil. Cover the pot and leave to cook on high heat till all the cocoyam lumps have dissolved. You can add more water if you feel that the soup is too thick.


  3. Add the ora (oha) leaves and leave to cook for about 5 minutes.


  4. Add salt to taste, stir and the soup is ready.


  5. Serve with any swallow of your choice, like garri, pounded yam, semovita, oatmeal swallow etc.
