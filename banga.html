BANGA

Nigerian Soup










INGREDIENTS



  1. Vegetable: Scent Leaves for Ofe Akwu or dried and crushed bitterleaves for Delta-style Banga Soup
  2. Chili Pepper
  3. Assorted Beef: Includes best cut, shaki (cow tripe)
  4. 1-2 medium chopped onions
  5. 1 kg Palm Fruits or 800g tinned Palm Fruit Concentrate
  6. Dry Fish
  7. Seasoning cubes
  8. Salt
  9. 1 teaspoon Ogiri Igbo
  10. 2 tablespoons of grounded crayfish






How To Prepare



  1. Extract the palm fruit concentrate from the palm fruits. If using the tinned palm fruit concentrate, open the tin and set aside, Set the pot of palm fruit extract on the stove and start cooking at high heat. Leave to boil till you notice come red oil at the surface of the Banga Stew. If you think that the Banga Soup is watery, cook till the soup has thickened to the consistency you like for your stews.

  2. Now, add the beef, dry fish and stock, the onions, crayfish and pepper and leave to boil very well.


  3. Add the scent leaves or other vegetable and salt to taste. Leave to simmer for about 2 mins. The Banga Soup is done.


  4. Serve with any swallow of your choice, like starch, garri, pounded yam, semovita, oatmeal swallow etc.
