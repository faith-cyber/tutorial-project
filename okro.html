OKRO

Nigerian Soup










INGREDIENTS



  1. Meat
  2. Stockfish
  3. Dry Fish
  4. 1-2 Chopped onions
  5. 3-4Scotch Bonnet Pepper
  6. Grounded Crayfish
  7. 3cups of palm oil
  8. 500g sliced okro
  9. Seasoning cubes
  10. Salt
  11. 80g Ugu leaves






How To Prepare



  1. Cut okro to your desired size and set aside.

  2. Wash the meat and stockfish, place in a medium-sized pot. Season with salt, 1 seasoning cube and 1/2 the onions. Add a little water and boil till they are tender.


  3. Soak the dry fish in boiling hot water for 2 minutes, drain and rinse in cold water. Add to the boiling meat.
  4. Add pepper, crayfish, palmoil, the rest of the onions and seasoning cube to the boiling meat, stir and taste, add salt.


  5. Cook for 15 minutes, then add okro, After 5 minutes, add ugu, stir and cook for 3 minutes, take off the heat.


  6. Serve with any swallow of your choice, like garri, pounded yam, semovita, oatmeal swallow etc.
